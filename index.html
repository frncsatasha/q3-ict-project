My Vision of my Future

My primary goal as of now is to finish school. To be totally honest, I don't have a certain profession that I'd like to have in the future; however, I'd like to work in the media. Up to now, I'm still weighing out which career to pursue: journalist, magazine editor, graphic designer or multimedia artist. Something around that field. Whatever the future holds for me, I just hope that it will be something I'm passionate about and can continue to hold onto for the rest of my life.

Besides that, I desire to fulfill some of my family's dreams. We often talk about moving abroad to live together. Perhaps in the United States where my grandma lives so that we can be near her. And as for me, I hope to at least contribute to supporting my family's needs, which include our dream house, car, and other basic necessities. Before all else, I hope to be financially stable enough to repay my parents and grandparents because if it wasn’t for them, I know I wouldn't be who I am today. I owe them bigtime!

Lastly, I aspire to travel around the world! New York City is at the top of my list. I've always wanted to visit NYC just like what I watch in the movies. Plus, I'd like to experience riding a helicopter and see how the city looks at night. I have always loved city lights! I've also always wanted to explore Europe, especially in Paris, France. I can't help but envision visiting this place every time I see pictures of it. Simply put, I find it to be incredibly artful and aesthetically pleasing. I also can never resist French pastries. I can't wait longer!
